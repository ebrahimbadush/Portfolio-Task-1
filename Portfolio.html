About Me

Ebrahim Badusha is a passionate graphic designer and videographer with a strong eye for visual storytelling and creative detail. He specializes in creating visually compelling designs and engaging video content that effectively communicate ideas and brand messages. With a keen interest in modern design trends and multimedia production, he continuously strives to enhance his skills and deliver high-quality creative work.
Ebrahim Badusha is currently pursuing a BCA Honours degree at Mar Baselios Institute of Technology and Science (MBITS), Nellimattom, where he is building a strong foundation in technology alongside his creative pursuits. By combining technical knowledge with artistic creativity, he aims to develop impactful digital experiences and grow as a versatile creative professional.
